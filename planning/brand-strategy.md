# Dr. Hump Brand Strategy & Website Planning

## Overview
This document outlines the brand strategy for doctorhump.com, a personal brand website representing three core pillars.

---

## Core Brand Pillars

### 1. Personal Life
- Personal stories, experiences, and perspectives
- Lifestyle and personal interests
- Human connection and authenticity
- Cincinnati connections and local presence

### 2. Artificial Intelligence
- AI expertise and thought leadership
- Projects and implementations
- Insights on AI technology trends
- Practical applications and use cases
- Educational content about AI

### 3. Case Race
- Case race participation and achievements
- Business case study analysis
- Competition strategies and insights
- Team collaboration and leadership
- Problem-solving methodologies

---

## Brand Identity
**Name:** Dr. Hump
**Domain:** doctorhump.com
**Tone:** Professional yet personal, combining academic credibility with authentic personality

---

## Website Vision
The website should integrate all three pillars seamlessly, showcasing:
- Who Dr. Hump is as a person
- Expertise and contributions in AI
- Experience and success in case race competitions

---

## Technical Infrastructure
- **Hosting:** Azure Static Web Apps
- **Deployment:** Automated via GitHub Actions
- **Repository:** GitHub (main branch auto-deploys)

---

## Future Considerations
- Content strategy for each pillar
- Visual design reflecting brand identity
- Navigation structure balancing all three areas
- Blog/articles section for ongoing content
- Portfolio/showcase sections
- Contact and social media integration
