## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Review Planning Questions
Read through [website-planning-questions.md](website-planning-questions.md) to understand what decisions need to be made.

### Step 2: Answer Questions
Fill out [website-answers.md](website-answers.md) with your responses. Take your time - these answers drive everything.

### Step 3: Review Testimonials Strategy
Look at [testimonials-strategy.md](testimonials-strategy.md) and decide how you want to display your 54 testimonials.

### Step 4: Content Gathering
Collect any existing materials:
- Professional photos
- Bio/about text
- Video content
- Links to inspiration websites
- Color/brand preferences

### Step 5: Move to Strategy Phase
Once questions are answered, we'll create:
- Site map
- Content outlines for each page
- Design mockups
- Development timeline
