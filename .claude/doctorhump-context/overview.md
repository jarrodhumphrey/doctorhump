## Website Mission

**One-sentence mission:**
"doctorhump.com is the ideal place to learn all about what is important to Jarrod Humphrey: applied generative AI expertise, Case Race, posting on social media, and his family"

### Primary Purpose
Enable people to **learn about Dr. Hump** - nothing for sale, no immediate monetization

### Primary Audiences
1. People who discover Dr. Hump on YouTube (Dr. Hump channel)
2. Students at Xavier University or in higher education looking to find him
3. Potential clients interested in hiring for AI purposes

### Desired Visitor Impression (after 5 minutes)
"That I'm a professional with a unique brand - interesting, cool, funny, with tons of applied AI expertise"

### Success Metric (1 year)
"If my personal brand flowed through that website" - people should understand what Dr. Hump is working on professionally: AI at Xavier, Case Race game, YouTube/social media

## Development Philosophy

### Iterative Approach
**Start simple, then keep adding to it. DON'T try to do everything at once.**

- Build incrementally
- Launch with basics, enhance over time
- Not trying to be perfect on day 1

### Writing Tone
- **Third person** (not first person "I")
- **Friendly but professional**
- Not too academic/boring
- Not overly marketing-focused
- Unique "Dr. Hump" personality throughout

### Visual Philosophy
- **Not SEO-focused** (at least not initially)
- **Minimal photos** of Jarrod - mostly graphics and text
- **Cool, interesting visuals** that make people say "wow"
- Professional but different from typical academic sites
